Quick Facts about Brew & Flour

Brew & Flour is located at 42, Race Course Road, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu 641018. The café serves specialty coffee and fresh in-house bakery items. Brew & Flour is open Monday to Thursday from 8 AM to 10 PM. On Fridays and Saturdays the café is open until 11 PM. On Sundays the café opens at 9 AM and closes at 9 PM. Walk-in customers are always welcome without a reservation. Free WiFi is available for all customers. The café is wheelchair accessible. All menu items are available for takeaway.
