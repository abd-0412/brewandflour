Our Story

Built on patience, folding butter, and searching for the perfect pour in Coimbatore.

Where the Perfect Bite Meets the Perfect Cup

Brew & Flour started with a simple idea: coffee should be enjoyed with something freshly baked. Not a packaged biscuit. Not a stale muffin. Something warm, flaky, and made that morning.

Our founder discovered specialty coffee while traveling through Coimbatore's hidden coffee estates. He learned about bean sourcing, roasting profiles, and the perfect pour. But something was missing — the perfect bite beside the perfect cup.

Then he met our baker. Trained in traditional French pastry, she believed croissants should take three days to make. Butter folded into dough. Rested. Folded again. Slowly, patiently.

They opened Brew & Flour on a quiet street. The first morning, they sold out of croissants by 9 AM. Today, we are where Coimbatore comes for quiet mornings, working afternoons, and conversations over coffee.

Our Core Philosophies

The driving elements behind every cup of espresso we pull and dough we fold.

Quality Coffee

We source beans directly from nearby estates in the Nilgiris, monitoring roast curves closely, pulling each shot under precise, professional conditions.

Fresh Baked Daily

We work on slow, heritage fermentation routines without artificial yeast triggers or packaged fats, creating flaky pastries in our open-view bakery space.

Cozy Community

We serve as the local Coimbatore salon where artists draft their next scripts, students review notes, and neighbors enjoy calm morning conversations.
